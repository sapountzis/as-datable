How to use

  • Follow the sequence: research users first, then design features that solve real problems.
  • Each item includes a success signal so you know when to move forward.
  • Start small with one focused AI feature before expanding to more complex applications.

1) User Research & Problem Validation

  • Tip: Many "AI problems" are actually UX problems. Fix the obvious issues first.

2) Feature Design & Planning

  • Tip: Design the human-AI collaboration, not just the AI feature in isolation.

3) Development & Integration

  • Tip: Ship something imperfect that users can try, then improve based on real usage.

4) Launch & Validation

  • Tip: Watch for users finding unexpected ways to use your AI feature.

5) Growth & Optimization

  • Tip: Great AI products get better with use—plan how your system learns from users.
